3 Cases For Workplace Diversity & Inclusion

We are often told that organizations should prioritize diversity internally — but most often wonder: Does diversity really pay off? It turns out the answer is yes, according to the most recent research! These videos highlight three central cases for adopting diversity, equity and inclusion in the professional workplace.

DEI Challenges Podcast

Organizations face several challenges in adopting Diversity & Inclusion efforts. Conflict, miscommunication, and trust issues often get in the way of organizational equity and inclusion. How do we ensure members of organizations and managers are cognizant of their affect on diverse groups and continue to remind themselves of their responsibility to be fair? In this podcast, Mikki and Eden discuss psychological concepts behind some of the obstacles organizations face in adopting and maintaining an inclusive culture.
